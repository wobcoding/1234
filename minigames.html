About

Welcome to the Retro Byte Blast!

This site is a retro-inspired collection of mini games, designed for fun, challenge, and a blast of 80's arcade nostalgia.

Features:

  • 🌈 Rainbow Typing Game: Test your typing speed and accuracy as words fall in a rainbow cascade!
  • ♟️ Simple Chess: Play classic chess with a friend, or try out chess puzzles!
  • 🧠 Memory Game: Flip cards and match pairs in a race against the clock!
  • ➕ Math Quiz Game: Solve math problems under time pressure!
  • 🎮 More games coming soon—each with unique retro flair!

All games are crafted with love for pixel art, neon colors, and the spirit of the arcade era.

Enjoy and stay tuned for updates!
